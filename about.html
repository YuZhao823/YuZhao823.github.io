About me

Yu Zhao - MFA Design Informatics

Yu is an interaction/user experience designer, with a background of Urban and Rual Planning, who is doing his master of fine art degree in the University of Edinburgh.

He aims to use digital and physical methods to explore the role of data throughout the paradigms of desktop publishing, world wide web and mobile computing, moving toward ubiquitous systems that involve environments and material artefacts.
